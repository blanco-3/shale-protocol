The brand logo as a crisp inline SVG; use in nav bars, headers, footers, loading states, and lockups.

```jsx
<StrataMark size={36} />
<StrataMark size={40} wordmark wordmarkStyle="caps" />        {/* nav lockup */}
<StrataMark size={56} wordmark color="var(--rock-200)" />     {/* serif lockup */}
```

The three strata colors are fixed brand values; only the wordmark color is themeable.
